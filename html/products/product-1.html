MASS REGENERATOR
High quality glutamine supplement from L-Glutamine with added taurine and vitamin B6
PRODUCT TYPE:
L-Glutamine
WEIGHT:
300 g
SERVING:
FLAVOURS:
MORE INFORMATIONS:
Glutamine is one of the most abundant amino acids in the human body, responsible for a range of functions from contributing the balance of our nitrogen levels and minimising muscle degradation to improving protein metabolism. During intensive training sessions, the body breaks down cell proteins which can result in a depletion of glutamine levels – which are needed for effective protein synthesis. Designed for the development of lean muscle mass, Mass Regenerator presents a breakthrough in sports nutrition, supplementing glutamine during periods of high intensity training or competition, allowing levels of this essential amino acid to remain sufficient to assist with the protein synthesis.
